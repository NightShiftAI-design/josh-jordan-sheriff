Core Priorities

These priorities reflect Josh’s experience in patrol operations, supervision, investigations, and crisis response — and his commitment to raising standards while strengthening community trust.

Professional Leadership

Lead by example and set clear expectations across the department. Build a strong command structure with real operational experience so every division runs effectively and consistently.

Accountability & Transparency

Earn trust through professionalism, consistent standards, and clear communication with the public. Raise expectations internally and ensure the Sheriff’s Office remains responsive to citizens.

Strong Patrol Operations

Improve day-to-day patrol readiness and focus on the calls that most impact families and neighborhoods. Support deputies with training, supervision, and clear direction in the field.

Better Crisis Response

Expand the Sheriff’s Office ability to handle crisis calls safely, professionally, and with the right resources. Strengthen partnerships that help reduce harm and improve outcomes.

Mental Health & CIT Approach

Continue building CIT-informed response methods and working relationships with local and regional providers to ensure the right approach is used when mental health is part of the call.

Respectful, Balanced Policing

Treat people with respect until the situation forces otherwise. Apply good judgment to each case, focusing on safety, lawful enforcement, and the right level of response for the circumstances.

What This Looks Like in Practice

A Sheriff’s Office that improves every day — through training, leadership, and a culture that takes pride in doing the job the right way.

Training That Matters

Ongoing, practical training focused on patrol tactics, de-escalation, investigations, and officer safety — paired with strong supervision.

Community Trust

A consistent, professional approach that citizens can rely on — where interactions are respectful, and decisions are accountable.

Operational Consistency

Clear policies, clear expectations, and leadership that ensures every division works together efficiently — from patrol to investigations.

A Commitment to Higher Standards

Josh Jordan does not settle for half effort. He believes high standards protect the public, strengthen officer performance, and build community trust. If elected Sheriff, he will work to raise expectations across the department and deliver professional service to every community in Rhea County.
